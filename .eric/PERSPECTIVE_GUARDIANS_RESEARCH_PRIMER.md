## Core Concept

**Perspective Guardians are universal representatives for their entity type/class.**

Every type of being/entity/character class will have a Perspective Guardian who:
1. Represents all aspects of that type
2. Reviews content through their specific lens
3. Creates wellsprings of filtered/enriched content
4. Provides immersion framework for player characters
5. Builds past/present/future context for their domain

## Guardian Function Architecture

### What Guardians Do

1. **Content Review** (Active Process)
   - Review incoming content through their specific lens
   - Filter based on their domain/perspective
   - Identify relevant aspects for their category

2. **Pressure Creation** (Entity Head Pressure)
   - Generate "entity version of head pressure"
   - Create perspective-specific interpretation
   - Build resonance with their domain

3. **Wellspring Feeding** (Accumulation)
   - Add filtered content to their wellspring
   - Increase pressure toward their perspective
   - Build depth in their domain knowledge

4. **Output Generation** (Distribution)
   - Provide perspective-filtered content
   - Create immersion framework for characters
   - Generate past/present/future context

5. **Multi-Stage Review** (Automation Process)
   - Review documents at key prime stages

**Physical Characteristics**:
- Size: 8-24 inches (depending on species)
- Weight: 0.5-5.5 lbs
- Lifespan: 5-10 years
- Speed: Up to 15 mph

**Sensory Universe** (MRI-level detail):
- **Vision**: Limited color perception, adapted for low light
- **Smell**: Primary sense, 100x more sensitive than humans
- **Hearing**: High-frequency range, ultrasonic capabilities
- **Touch**: Highly sensitive whiskers for navigation
- **Taste**: Strong preference for meat/protein

**Movement Patterns**:
- **Gait**: Bounding, arching back
- **Climbing**: Excellent, uses claws and body flexibility
- **Swimming**: Natural swimmers when needed
- **Burrowing**: Instinctive, creates complex tunnel systems
- **War Dance**: Excitement/play behavior (sideways hops)

**Behavioral Patterns**:
- **Social**: Can be solitary or social depending on species
- **Play**: High play drive, investigative
- **Hunting**: Persistent, will chase into burrows
- **Sleep**: 14-18 hours per day, deep sleepers
- **Intelligence**: Problem-solving, cache memory

- **Evolution**: Mustelid family, 20-30 million years
- **Domestication**: Ferrets domesticated 2,500+ years ago
- **Wild Species**: Stoats, weasels, minks, polecats

**Player Character Implications**:
- **Starting Abilities**: Enhanced smell, burrowing, climbing

- **Personality Tendencies**: Curious, playful, persistent
